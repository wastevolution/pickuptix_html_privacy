FAQ

Common questions

How do I sign in?

Wearing uses Sign in with Apple and Sign in with Google. There’s no separate password to remember — just tap your preferred option on the welcome screen.

How do I add clothes to my wardrobe?

Tap the add button, take a photo (or pick one from your library), then set the category, color, and any tags. Your item is saved instantly and synced to the cloud.

How do outfit suggestions work?

Wearing combines the items in your wardrobe with your local weather to suggest looks that fit the day. The more of your closet you add, the better the suggestions get.

Is my data backed up?

Yes. Your wardrobe, outfits, and lists are securely stored in the cloud and synced across your devices, so you won’t lose anything if you switch phones.

Why does the app ask for location and photo access?

Location is used only to show local weather for outfit planning. Photos/camera let you add pictures of your clothing. You can manage both anytime in your device settings.

How do I delete my account or data?

You can delete individual items in the app, or remove your account entirely from Settings — this deletes your associated data. You can also email us and we’ll handle the request.
